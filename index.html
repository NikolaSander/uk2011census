Migration Flows in the United Kingdom from the 2011 Census

Explore migration flows by age between NUTS-1 and NUTS-2 regions of the UK from the 2011 Census.
Click on a region to discover flows at the NUTS-2 level. Note that flows with less than 1,500 migrants are not shown.

by Nikola Sander, John Stillwell and Nik Lomax

This visualisation complements Chapter 16 in the "Handbook of Census Resources, Methods and Applications: Unlocking the UK 2011 Census".
